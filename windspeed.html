Latitude vs. Windspeed

vs. Wind Speed

Within the northern hemisphere, there is an increase in wind speed in approximately eight locations between the latitudes of 60 and 80 degrees. Aside from these outliers, there does not appear to be a strong connection between latitude and wind speed. Other factors are likely more important, such as proximity to a coast, elevation above sea level, or location in relation to mountain valleys, among other reasons.
